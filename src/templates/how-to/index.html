How to load a Podverse playlist in any podcast app

1) Click the "RSS Feed" button on a playlist page.

2) Copy the RSS Feed URL for that playlist. NOTE: this URL must have ?rssFeed at the end.

3) Open a podcast app. Most podcast apps have an "Add Podcast Manually" option. Select that, then paste the RSS Feed URL, and click save.

4) The playlist should now be added in your podcast apps. This playlist will update over time as new episodes or clips are added to it.
