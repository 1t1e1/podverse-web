Terms

Podverse is a free and open source (AGPLv3) app for sharing highlights and playlists of podcasts.

Podverse does not host or edit podcaster images or audio files. All images and audio files are referenced from the podcaster's public RSS feed.

Podverse hosts metadata about the podcast (e.g. podcast title, episode title, episode description) shared in the podcaster's public RSS feed.

Podverse does not put advertisements next to or within podcaster content, or anywhere on podverse.fm, without the podcaster's permission.

Podverse will take down any podcaster's content from podverse.fm at the podcaster's request.

Podverse will not sell user data.
