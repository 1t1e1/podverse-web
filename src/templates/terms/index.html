Terms

Podverse:

1) Creates free and open source technology for listening to and sharing podcasts.

2) Hosts metadata about the podcast (e.g. podcast title, episode title, episode description) found in the podcaster's public RSS feed.

3) Does not host or edit podcaster images or audio files. All images and audio files are referenced from URLs in the podcaster's public RSS feed.

4) Does not put advertisements next to or within podcaster content, or anywhere on podverse.fm, without the podcaster's permission.

5) Will take down any podcaster's content from podverse.fm at the podcaster's request.

6) Will not sell user data.
